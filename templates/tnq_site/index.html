We are a student group dedicated to photography, design, and crafting the yearbook of the Massachusetts Instutute of Technology.

  • Buy a book to remember the days you spent at the institute.
  • Support us to keep the club growing and learning.
  • Seniors, get your portraits in the book.
  • Living groups, schedule your group photo with us.
  • Join a thriving community of MIT photographers and designers. Learn how to use an SLR or a large format camera. Conquer Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. Develop film. Checkout a camera. Make friends.
  • Hire a photographer or two from our community to photograph your next event. We provide services for photo booths and studio photography.
